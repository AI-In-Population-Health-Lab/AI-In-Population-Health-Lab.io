Lab News

September, 2024

We developed a CNN structure that compares the ability of semantic embeddings from LLMs of BERT, BioBERT, Med-BERT, and OpenAI embedding models to classify the Influenza. The corresponding code package has been released and is available at CNN_package.

September, 2024

Dr. Ye has joined as a co-investigator on the newly funded grant, R24GM153920 , for the Models of Infectious Disease Agent Study (MIDAS) Coordination Center, led by Principal Investigator Dr. Harry Hochheiser.

June, 2024

Our paper, Online Transfer Learning for RSV Case Detection (Yiming as the first author) has received the Best Paper Award in the Analytics Track. Yuhe provided an oral presentation on behalf of the team at the IEEE ICHI 2024 in Orlando, FL.

May, 2024

Dr. Ye coauthored the paper A Bayesian System to Detect and Track Outbreaks of Influenza-Like Illnesses Including Novel Diseases: Algorithm Development and Validation, which is recently published.
